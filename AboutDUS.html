Our Mission.

"What we achieve inwardly will change outer reality." ~Plutarch



    Composer and producer, Thomas Prislac, partnered with celebrated percussionist and audio engineer Benjamin Engen, to found DUS Multimedia Production Studio LLC in 2014. Having noticed a need for local artists to have their work professionally documented and polished, this versatile and tech-savvy media studio was created with the mission of providing a safe and educational space for those whose voice might not otherwise be heard. DUS LLC performs outreach for artists of all types in every genre to learn more about modern recording and production best practices and how technology can bring the process home. In their first year of operation, DUS LLC scored for film, artist promotions, and provided production services in film and sound design.
    DUS LLC offers competitively priced services with multiple payment methods including revenue sharing, and financing. They also helped many area artist repair low quality recordings through their full service audio-restoration center. Mr. Prislac and Mr. Engen, through DUS Multimedia Production Studio LLC, both hope to provide the foundation on which the next generation of artists will stand up and have their work recognized.
